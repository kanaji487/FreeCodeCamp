Gawr Gura

Gawr Gura profile

Gawr Gura is an English Virtual YouTuber associated with hololive, as part of its English (EN) branch first generation of Vtubers alongside Ninomae Ina'nis, Takanashi Kiara, Watson Amelia, and Mori Calliope.

Gura is a descendant of the Lost City of Atlantis, who swam to Earth while saying, "It's so boring down there LOLOLOL!" She bought her clothes (and her shark hat) in the human world and she really loves them. In her spare time, she enjoys talking to marine life. Gura is over 9,000 years old. She forgot her exact age some time around 5,000s, and when asked gives a random number in the 9,000s range.

Gawr Gura history

  • 23 April 2020 - Auditions for an English-language division of hololive were announced.
  • 12 September 2020 - Gura's debut stream.
  • 20 October 2020 - Gura reach 100,000 new subscribers.
  • 22 October 2020 - Gura reach 1M subscribers.
  • 30 November 2020 - Gura reach 1.5M subscribers.
  • 17 January 2021 - Gura reached 2,000,000 subscribers on her YouTube channel, being the first hololive member to do so and the second VTuber ever to reach that milestone after Kizuna Ai.
  • 22 January 2021 - Gura celebrated her 2,000,000 subscriber milestone with a karaoke stream.
  • 1 April 2021 - All holoMyth members revealed new chibi models based on popular fan designs.
  • 30 April 2021 - Hololive's Twitter account announced that every member of holoMyth would get their second 2D costumes.
  • 6 May 2021 - Gura debuted her second 2D costume.
  • 18 June 2021 - Gura reached 1 million Twitter followers.
  • 20 June 2021 - Gura celebrated her birthday with a Jaws movie watch-along and a birthday stream in which she released merchandis.
  • 22 June 2021 - Gura released her first original song "REFLECT" on YouTube. It surpassed 1,000,000 views in 10 hours and 25 minutes after its premiere.
  • 4 July 2021 - Gura reached 3,000,000 subscribers on her YouTube channel, being the first hololive member, as well as the first VTuber in the world, to reach this milestone. Earlier on that same day, her song "REFLECT" surpassed 5,000,000 views on her YouTube channel.
